# Operators

## Perturbation Operator

P(S)

changes the biological state.

---

## Mirror Operator

M(S)

represents endogenous mirror dynamics.

---

## Recovery Operator

R(S)

describes recovery toward equilibrium.

---

## Adaptation Operator

A(S)

describes biological adaptation after perturbation.

---

## Observation Operator

O(S)

maps biological states into measurable signals.

Example

O(S)=Fluorescence

O(S)=Absorbance

O(S)=FTIR spectrum
